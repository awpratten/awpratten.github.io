title: Game Jam Sound Design
---

I've long held an interest in sound design.   I am very aware that developing a good sound design can make or break movies or games.  Good sound design sets the right mood and tone.   It can make the visuals seem more immersive.   Poor design can make things feel very bland or unfinished.

![LD58 - This Rocks](/images/posters/thisRocks.png)
I have participated in two [Ludum Dare](https://ldjam.com/) game jams.   Ludum Dare 58 was my first attempt.   The theme for that game jam was "collector".   The team decided to develop a game that had a character walking along the beach looking for interesting rocks.   The game can be found here https://ldjam.com/events/ludum-dare/58/this-rocks

![LD59 - Almost Empty](/images/posters/almostEmpty.png)

Ludum Dare 59 had the theme "signals".   The team developed a game where your character flies from planet to planet delivering packages.   The game can be found here https://ldjam.com/events/ludum-dare/59/almost-empty
