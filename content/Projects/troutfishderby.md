# Members:

* Warren Pratten - Guitar, FX, Loops
* [Alex Schmoll](https://vimeo.com/occasionaljake) - Synths & Electronics

Alex and I met through the [Magic Daffodils](/Projects/magicdaffodils).   I think it was pretty apparent quickly that there was some musical chemistry between us.   Since 2024 we've been getting together semi-regularly at his studio to jam.  Alex calls it "live-in-the-studio music making".  We record everything and the music is fantastic.   Long ambient pieces that have movement.   Exactly the kind of music I like to play.

We have decided to release our music under the project name *Trout Fish Derby* which is a crazy name that has nothing to do with anything.
